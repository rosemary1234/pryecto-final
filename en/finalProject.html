Digital Manufacturing Project

For the next project we focus on a problem that occurs in each of the homes. The vast majority of people are collectors of alcoholic beverages such as wines, whiskey and rums, among others. We can classify them in two; some collect them for the type of year and others for having a drink on celebratory dates. This means that each family has a special place for this type of drinks, but it is not so because everyone would like to have a minibar, these are placed in inappropriate places, exposing them to what is destroyed, falls, among others. That's why we want to create and materialize this product that is the portavins for all the families that do not have a place for their wines or any other drink.
