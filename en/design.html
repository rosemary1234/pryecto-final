design Thiking

Observing and knowing the work that a painter performs, we realize one of the most frequent problems he faces when painting is worth the redundadncia. When performing his work, the painter not only faces physical problems but also needs that happen at the moment of performing his work. The problems that we have been able to observe in general is that when you finish painting you have a muscular fatigue because of the position you are in at the time of doing this action and the lack of security that you would have when climbing structures that may be dangerous and fatal such as the chair, benches, stairs, among others. The needs we have been able to observe are: when one wants to paint but can not reach very high places such as a wall or walls, it requires structures since the apparatus with which it paints is very short and the time it takes to perform said work. Being able to give a tentative solution to the problem would be to create and facilitate the painter a tool that is suitable for work to avoid possible accidents and that can reduce the time of execution at work.
